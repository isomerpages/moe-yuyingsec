<h2>National Police Cadet Corps</h2>
<h3>Objectives</h3>
<p>The National Police Cadet Corps (NPCC) aims to develop our students into
disciplined, responsible, and resilient individuals. Through drills, leadership
training, community engagement, and law awareness programmes, cadets cultivate
a strong sense of civic duty and teamwork. NPCC instills core values such
as integrity, loyalty, and courage, shaping confident leaders who serve
the school and community with pride.</p>
<h3>Accomplishments</h3>
<p>Unit Overall Proficiency Award (2024), Distinction</p>
<p>Unit Overall Proficiency Award (2023), Distinction</p>
<p>Unit Overall Proficiency Award (2022), Distinction</p>
<p>2025 Uniformed Group Social Media Competition, Honourable mention</p>
<p>2024 Youth Against Scams Social Media Competition (Secondary), 3rd Place</p>
<p>2024 .22 Inter-Unit Revolver Competition (Boys), 2nd Place</p>
