<h2>Chinese Dance</h2>
<h3>Objectives</h3>
<p>At Yuying Chinese Dance (YYCD), members explore the beauty of Chinese
dance while building skills through posture and breathing exercises. They
learn teamwork, confidence, and stage presence, while fostering a love
for Chinese culture and rhythmic expression. Throughout their journey,
dancers gain valuable performance experiences in school events such as
the Sec 1 Orientation concert, Lunar New Year celebrations, Speech Day,
and Open House. They also showcase their talents in the biennial Singapore
Youth Festival and other external platforms. YYCD nurtures passion, discipline,
and respect, preparing dancers to perform gracefully and confidently.</p>
<h3>Accomplishments</h3>

<p>Performance at Paya Lebar National Day Celebration 2023</p>
<p>Participation in the 2024 National Day Parade (NDP)</p>
