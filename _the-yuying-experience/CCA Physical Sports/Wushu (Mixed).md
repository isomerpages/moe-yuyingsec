<h2>Wushu (Mixed)</h2>
<h3>Objectives</h3>
<p>Wushu instills discipline, grit, and craftsmanship through the mastery
of the art form. Students build physical agility and mental resilience
while learning traditional forms and weaponry. Through school performances
and National School Games, they gain confidence and teamwork to grow as
focused, skilled individuals committed to excellence.</p>
