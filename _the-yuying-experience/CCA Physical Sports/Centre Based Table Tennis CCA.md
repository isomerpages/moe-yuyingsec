<h2>Centre Based Table Tennis CCA</h2>
<p>The Cluster S6 Table Tennis CCA is a collaboration among four schools:
Yuying Secondary School, Bartley Secondary School, Peicai Secondary School,
and St. Gabrielâ€™s Secondary School.</p>
<h3>Objectives</h3>
<p>1) Enhance social interaction by promoting teamwork, collaboration, and
sportsmanship among players from the 4 different schools.</p>
<p>2) Cultivate self and team discipline and perseverance through CCA-related
activities and consistent practice;</p>
<p>3) Develop a competitive spirit through participation in tournaments and
friendly matches;</p>
<p>4) Fostering a lifelong appreciation for table tennis as a recreational
and competitive sport through the mastering of technical and tactical skills;</p>
<p>5) Develop leadership traits through various leadership positions and
training opportunities;</p>
<p>6) Encourage a healthy lifestyle by promoting regular physical activity
and improving physical fitness.</p>
<h3>Accomplishments</h3>
<p>2025 National School Games Table Tennis Tournament.</p>
<p>(Preliminary Round) Top in Group stage.</p>
<p>(Second Round - League 2) Second in Group Stage.</p>
