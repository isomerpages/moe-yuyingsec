<h2>Sepak Takraw (Boys)</h2>
<h3>Objectives</h3>
<p><strong>1. Character Through Commitment</strong>
</p>
<p>We instil discipline, resilience, and grit on and off the court.</p>
<p><strong>2. Teamwork Through Inclusivity</strong>
</p>
<p>We nurture a team culture built on trust, respect and unity across all
backgrounds.</p>
<p><strong>3. Growth Through Sport</strong>
</p>
<p>We create a safe space for students to grow in confidence, emotional strength,
and identity.</p>
<h3>Accomplishments</h3>
<p><strong>Team</strong>
</p>
<p>2025 National School Games Sepak takraw B Division Boys Championship</p>
<p>2025 National School Games Sepak takraw C Division Boys Championship</p>
<p>2025 2nd Place for CCA placing in Soaring Over Adversity Mass Run</p>
<p><strong>Individual</strong>
</p>
<p>2025 Compassionate Leader Award (Anaqi Fauzan Bin Faizal 4U)</p>
<p>2025 Compassionate Leader Award (Javier Teo Zhi En 4U)</p>
