<h2>Volleyball (Girls)</h2>
<h3>Objectives</h3>
<p>1. To cultivate a strong foundational understanding of volleyball through
the application of essential game rules and strategies.</p>
<p>2. To develop students’ technical proficiency across a range of skill
levels—from novice to advanced—by honing core techniques such as service,
footwork, receiving, and spiking.</p>
<p>3. To instill key sporting values including sportsmanship, fair play,
teamwork, and self-discipline, fostering both character development and
a positive team culture.</p>
