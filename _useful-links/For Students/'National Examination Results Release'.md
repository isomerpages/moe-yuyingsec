<h4><strong>RELEASE OF THE 2025 GCE O-LEVEL EXAMINATION RESULTS</strong></h4>
<p>Dear Parents / Guardians,</p>
<p>The results of the 2025 GCE O-Level Examination will be released on Wednesday,
14 January 2026, from 2.00 p.m. onwards.</p>
<p>Students are to report to the School Hall by 1.30 p.m. on the same day.</p>
<p>Please note that students are not required to be in school uniform. They
may report to school in appropriate home attire. All students are expected
to adhere to school grooming standards. This includes having no visible
facial or body piercings and removing all jewellery before reporting to
school. Appropriate footwear should also be worn.</p>
<p>Parents / guardians who are accompanying their child/ward are advised
that parking within the school compound is limited. Parking is available
at the nearby HDB car park.</p>
<p>Students who are unwell are advised to seek medical attention and not
report to school to collect their results. Students who are unable to collect
their results in person may appoint a proxy to collect a physical copy
of the results on their behalf.</p>
<p>Proxies are required to produce the relevant documents for the school’s
verification. Please click the following link to download the proxy form:
<br><a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/-/media/files/post-secondary/2026-jae/jae-proxy-form.pdf</a>
</p>
