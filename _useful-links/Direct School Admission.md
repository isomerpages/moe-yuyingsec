# Direct School Admission Exercise for 2026

  

#### **Join Yuying: Where Future Leaders Soar!**

At Yuying, we inspire.&nbsp;
 
Our students, the Eagles, are nurtured to be **Enterprising, Adaptable, Gracious, and Leaders of the Future** through a transformative learning experience that blends **deep learning, well-being, and resilience.
**
### Why Choose Yuying?

*   Signature "Yuying Changemakers" Programme    
Unlock your potential with our talent development initiatives, designed to cultivate self-directed leaders and skilful practitioners in your field of passion and strength.

  

*   Holistic Growth  
We foster curiosity, empathy, and resilience to empower students to thrive in an ever-changing world.

  

*   Strong Parent-School Partnership
    Together, we build a foundation for lifelong success for our Yuying Changemakers - you.

### Calling All Visionary Students!

Seize the opportunity to join Yuying through the **Direct Schools Admission (DSA)** exercise. Stand out as a **Changemaker** and embark on a journey where ambition meets purpose.

  

**Your Future Starts Here—Apply Now!**

(Successful DSA applicants will be enrolled in our prestigious "Yuying Changemakers" programme.)

  

#**SoarWithYuying**

Let’s shape the leaders of tomorrow — **together**.

  

We welcome all current Primary Six students (or equivalent) to apply for 2026 admission to our school as **Yuying Changemakers** through the DSA-Sec exercise. We offer Singaporeans and foreign students who fulfil the minimum criteria for secondary school admission, have specific talents, and meet one or more of the following criteria:

  

Sports and Games \- Volleyball (Girls)

Sports and Games \- Wushu (Boys and Girls)

Performing Arts \- Concert Band (Boys and Girls)

Community Youth Leadership \-&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Passionate leaders (Boys and Girls)
