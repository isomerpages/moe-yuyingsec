Parenting Tips
==============

         

1\. [MOE School Bag](https://www.schoolbag.edu.sg/secondary/)

Get updates on the ins and outs of what is happening in our education scene to support your parenting journey with your child.

2\. [Parenting for Wellness Toolkit](https://parentingforwellness.hpb.gov.sg/)

Access this toolkit to learn how to:

*   build strong parent-child relationships,
*   strengthen your child/ward's mental well-being and emotional resilience, and
*   parent effectively in the digital age.

This toolkit is jointly developed by MOE, MSF and HPB.

3\. [SG CyberSafe Programme](https://www.csa.gov.sg/our-programmes/cybersecurity-outreach/sg-cyber-safe-students/#e355af3eefc69d5cc5f7d0328a452ac5)

Learn how to support your child in navigating the cyberspace safely and adopting good cyber hygiene practices.

4\. [Digital for Life Resources](https://www.digitalforlife.gov.sg/learn/resources)

Access resources to learn more about setting up parental controls on digital devices, and responsible use of Generative AI and social media.

5\. [MOE Cyber Wellness Parent Resources](https://go.gov.sg/cwresources-parents)

Learn more about how to keep your child safe online. This is a mobile-friendly site containing quick access to tips and guides for parents on cyber wellness.
