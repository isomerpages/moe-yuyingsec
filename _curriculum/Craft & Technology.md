<h1>Craft &amp; Technology</h1>
<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>Every Yuying student a critical and inventive thinker.</p>
<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>Empowering students with the grit and skills to communicate their innovative
ideas.</p>
<h3>Philosophy</h3>
<p>Teaching C&amp;T subjects is about nurturing curious individuals who values
empathy and innovation.</p>
<h3>Key Programmes &amp; Competitions</h3>
<p><strong>Coursework-based Learning</strong>
</p>
<p>Students apply empathy-driven learning for real-world issues.</p>
<p>Farm to table: Students engage in hands-on experience by transforming
their own grown vegetables to dishes on the plate.</p>
<p>Edible STEM+: Students are exposed to future food innovation through 3D
food printer.</p>
<p>Alternative Protein: Students are exposed to STEM and Food Sustainability
through practical hands-on approach.</p>
<p>Toy design: Students participate in a hands-on experience to design and
construct their own motion-based toys.</p>
<p>Hybrid creature design: Students create a tunnel book to bring their fun
and interactive hybrid character designs alive.</p>
<p><strong>Stretch programmes:</strong>
</p>
<p>Opportunities for high-progress learners to challenge themselves through
showcase and competitions:</p>
<p>1) ApLM@Poly</p>
<p>2) Artist-in-school programme (AISS)</p>
<p>3) Urban sketch session (Art Elective Programme Festival)</p>
<p>4) Youth Challenge (ALP)</p>
<p>5) Tan Kah Kee Young Inventor Award (ALP and Sustainability)</p>
